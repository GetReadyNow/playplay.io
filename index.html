Play

It's easy to join. Create a channel (#pong, #chess or #pool) and invite the bot (@pongbot, @chessbot or @poolbot) to it.

We'll just say @bot below, but you should address your bot as @pongbot, @chessbot or @poolbot.

Every user that wants to play says @bot register (so @pongbot register, @chessbot register or @poolbot register) ...

... then challenges someone to a match with @bot challenge @opponent

... accept a game with @bot accept and go play ...

... the loser says @bot lost, in a draw both say @bot draw.

Check your rankings with @bot leaderboard 10 and @bot rank.

Try @bot help for help, that is @pongbot help, @chessbot help or @poolbot help.

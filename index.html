The Games

It's easy to join. Create a channel (#pong or #chess) and invite the bot (@pongbot or chessbot) to it.

Each user that wants to play should say @pongbot register or @chessbot register ...

... then challenges someone to a match ...

... the loser says pongbot lost, in a draw both say chessbot draw.

Check your rankings with "pongbot leaderboard" and "pongbot rank".

See detailed usage for other commands.
